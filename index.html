Vihren peak - discover the raw beauty of God's Peak.

Vihren peak is located in the northern part of Pirin mountain. With its height of 2,914 m, Mount Vihren ranks second after Mount Musala (2,925 m) in Bulgaria and third after the Greek Mount Mitikas (2,917 m) on the Balkan Peninsula. irin Mountain is one of the most difficult mountains to climb in Bulgaria, and the same applies to Mount Vihren. The summit of Pirin can be reached by several routes.

Legends of the Mountain

According to one of the legends about Vihren, the marble throne of the main god of the Slavs - Perun - was located at the top. God Perun was the lord of storms and thunder. Mount Pirin is named after the thunder god.

According to another legend, a metal post or stake with a metal ball on it was driven into the very top, which was used by Noah to tie the boat.
